Prices

Standard Plan - $5/mo

Basic Plan features include:

  • unlimited searches to mentors and entrepreneurs.
  • five personal messages to mentors and entrepreneurs a week.
  • Ability to create a detailed user account
  • Unlimited global questions
Advanced Plan - $10/mo

Advanced Plan features include:

  • Standard Plan features.
  • twenty personal messages to mentors and entrepreneurs a week.
  • Mentor suggestions.
  • Curated business tips.
  • Mentor meetups.
Ultimate Plan - $20/mo

Ultimate Plan features include:

  • Standard Plan features.
  • Advanced Plan features.
  • Ability to meet with your mentors over face chat weekly.
  • Set up investor opotunities.
  • unlimited access to everything Mentor Me has to offer.
