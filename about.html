About Us

What Were About

Founded in 2019, Mentor Me consists of six very ambitious people set out on a journey to better young entrepreneurs lives. Once young entrepreneurs ourselves, we learned how to become successful the hard ways and our aim is to make this process a whole lot easier by connecting amateur entrepreneurs to more developed entrepreneurs.

Our Mission

Our mission here is to take that hopeless feeling of jumping into entrepreneurship out of the equation and allow people to take this endevour head on and achieve great things.

Why Us

We know what its like having the thirst for knowledge and not being able to quench that thirst. What we offer here is that "quench". The opportunity to connect with the deep knowledge of entrepreneurship is lacking in our society and its very hard to find mentors. This app does it all for you.
